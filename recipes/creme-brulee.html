Crème Brûlée

Description

A favorite of Amélie Poulain made famous in the eponymous film, crème brûlée, when made properly, is sheer perfection: A sweet, vanilla-infused custard is just barely set in the oven before being chilled until set and smooth.

Ingredients

  • Vanilla
  • Custard Cream
  • Ramekin
  • Sugar
